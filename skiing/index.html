Skiing

What Skiing Means To Me

To me skiing is the best winter sport. I started skiing when I was about 4 yrs old. I've been skiing every year since then. My favorite resort to go skiing is Alta. My favorite thing to do while skiing is skiing in powder. I find this extremely fun when theres a lot of powder. I've skied across almost all the main resorts in the SLC and Park City area.

Time Line

  • Started Skiing at Alta when I was 4.
  • Stayed at Alta for the next year.
  • The next year we got Brighton season passes.
  • Stayed with Brighton passes for the next 3 ish years.
  • For the next 3 years we got Ikon passes so we could move to different resorts to ski.
  • After those three years we went back to Alta passes
  • Then we stayed with Alta passes for the next 3 years.
  • This year was a little different. We wanted to be able to go to Alta and more resorts, so we got Alta season passes, as well as Ikon base passes.
